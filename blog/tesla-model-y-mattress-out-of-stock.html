Tesla Model Y Air Mattress Out of Stock? Here Are the Best Alternatives

The official Tesla Model Y Air Mattress is frequently unavailable — it sells out fast and restocks unpredictably. These 5 alternatives fit the Model Y just as well, are available right now, and require no Tesla account to buy.

By EV Mattress Guide · Updated July 2026 · Source: Tesla Shop (shop.tesla.com), verified July 2026

Why It's So Often Out of Stock

Tesla sells the Model Y Air Mattress exclusively through the Tesla Shop, and only to verified Tesla owners. That combination — a single sales channel plus a fast-growing owner base — means stock disappears quickly whenever it's replenished. There's no workaround: no third-party retailers carry it, and there's no Amazon listing.

What Any Replacement Needs to Match

Dimensions that actually fit. The Model Y cargo area with seats folded is approximately 74–76" long × 50–52" wide. Any replacement needs to fill this space comfortably without gaps or overhang that creates uneven sleeping.
Compatibility with Camp Mode. Camp Mode keeps the Model Y's climate control running overnight on battery. This means your mattress doesn't need a high R-value for warmth — the HVAC handles that. You're selecting primarily for comfort and fit.
Available without a Tesla account. Every alternative below ships to anyone, today, with no ownership verification or waiting for restocks.

How to Get Notified When Tesla Restocks

If you specifically want the official Tesla mattress and can wait:

1
On the Tesla Shop product page, use the "Email me when this item is restocked" form. Tesla will notify you by email when it returns — keep your Tesla account login ready since stock sells out within hours.
2
Watch r/TeslaLounge on Reddit — community members post restock alerts within minutes of stock appearing. Search "air mattress" in the subreddit to find recent threads.
3
Check the Tesla Motors Club forum (teslamotorsclub.com) — another active community that tracks Tesla accessories including the mattress.
