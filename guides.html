🔌 EV Camper Summary

If you have Camp Mode or climate control, you can use any mattress type year-round regardless of R-value — the HVAC does the insulation work. For cold-weather camping without climate control, choose R-5 or higher. For summer or EV camping with climate, R-2 to R-4 is plenty and saves weight.

R-Value measures thermal resistance — how well a mattress insulates you from the cold ground or vehicle floor. Higher = warmer. This matters differently in EVs than traditional camping.

💡 The EV-Specific Advantage

Traditional campers must rely on mattress insulation to survive cold nights. EV campers with Camp Mode have a backup: the HVAC maintains temperature all night on battery. This means you can use a lighter, lower R-value mattress and still sleep warm. A R-2 mattress + Tesla Camp Mode often outperforms a R-7 mattress in a traditional tent.
