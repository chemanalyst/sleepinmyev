Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what cookies are, which ones sleepinmyev.com uses, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners.

What We Currently Use

sleepinmyev.com uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site — which pages are most useful, where people come from, and how the site can be improved. We do not use advertising or retargeting cookies.

We only load Google Analytics after you give consent via the cookie banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set.

Affiliate Links

When you click a "View product →" link and visit REI, Amazon, or Luno, those retailers may set their own cookies on your device for affiliate tracking, personalisation, and analytics. We earn a small commission if you make a purchase — at no extra cost to you. Those retailers' cookie and privacy policies apply once you leave our site.

Your Consent

When you first visit the site, a banner asks you to accept or decline cookies. If you accept, Google Analytics loads and begins collecting anonymised data. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set. You can change your choice at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site, or by using your browser's cookie controls.

Your Controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or be notified about cookies. Note that blocking cookies may affect how some websites you visit work — but since we set none, it won't affect this site.
