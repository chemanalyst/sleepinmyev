Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This policy explains what cookies are, which ones sleepinmyev.com uses, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners.

What We Currently Use

sleepinmyev.com is a static site with no user accounts, no login system, and no advertising. We currently set no cookies of our own. The site runs entirely in your browser using local JavaScript — nothing is sent to our servers.

Affiliate Links

When you click a "View product →" link and visit REI, Amazon, or Luno, those retailers may set their own cookies on your device for affiliate tracking, personalisation, and analytics. Those retailers' cookie and privacy policies apply once you leave our site.

Future Changes

If we add analytics or other tracking in the future, we will update this policy and add a cookie consent banner before any tracking cookies are set. We will always ask for your permission before any non-essential cookies are stored on your device.

Your Controls

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block, delete, or be notified about cookies. Note that blocking cookies may affect how some websites you visit work — but since we set none, it won't affect this site.
