Why EV Camping Is Different

Standard mattress reviews ignore what actually matters for electric vehicle owners.

Range Impact Is Tiny — But Real

A 9 lb mattress costs under 0.1% range on most EVs — less than 0.25 miles. Your roof box and driving speed matter far more than mattress weight.

📐

Sleeping Length Varies by 28 Inches

From the Chevrolet Bolt's 60" to the VW ID.Buzz's 88", the difference is enormous. We track exact sleeping dimensions for all 22 EVs so you know before you buy.

🌡️

Overnight Climate Changes the Insulation Math

Tesla Camp Mode, Kia/Hyundai Utility Mode, and Rivian Camp Mode all maintain temperature overnight on battery. Lower R-value mattresses work perfectly with these systems.

🔌

Your Height Determines Which EV Fits

A 6'5"+ person won't fit in a Chevrolet Bolt (60"). And despite the VW ID.4's 77" length, its 37" floor width means solo sleepers only. Our height-based finder shows which EVs work for you before you book a campsite.
